# Dataset Notes

Future datasets must be reviewed before training.

- Use only datasets with clear licensing and consent for the intended research or product purpose.
- Do not use or create attractiveness labels.
- Do not create shaming labels.
- Do not claim exact body-fat percentage unless validated against an appropriate clinical or lab method.
- Prefer labels and tasks around photo quality, pose consistency, segmentation, posture normalization, and user-relative change.
- Evaluate demographic coverage, lighting variance, camera distance, clothing variance, and pose variance.
- Keep Kaggle credentials outside the repo. Never commit `kaggle.json`.
- Store raw datasets outside source control.
- Document model limitations in the app and in release notes.
